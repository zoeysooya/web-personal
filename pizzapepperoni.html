How to Make Pepperoni Pizza

Materials:

  • 250 grams of high-protein wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon of instant yeast
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of granulated sugar
  • 150 ml of warm water
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 100 grams of pizza sauce
  • 150 grams of mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 50 grams of pepperoni (thinly sliced)
  • ½ teaspoon of dried oregano

Steps:

  1. First, combine the high-protein flour, instant yeast, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Next, gradually add the warm water while kneading until the dough starts to come together.
  3. Then, add the olive oil and knead again for about 10 minutes until the dough is elastic.
  4. After that, cover the dough with a clean cloth and let it rest for 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  5. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 200°C.
  6. Now, lightly flour a clean surface. Roll out the dough into a circle with a thickness of about ½ cm.
  7. Carefully transfer the dough to a baking sheet that has been greased or lined with baking paper.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the surface of the dough.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce
  10. Arrange the pepperoni slices over the cheese.
  11. Sprinkle dried oregano to add aroma and flavor.
  12. Carefully place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  13. Finally, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before cutting.
  14. Cut the pizza into slices and serve while it's warm.
