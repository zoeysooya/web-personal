How to Make Fried Rice

Materials:

  • 1 plate of cooked white rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 shallots (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon of oyster sauce (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ½ teaspoon of ground pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of chicken powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • 3 meatballs (thinly sliced)
  • 2 shrimps (peeled)
  • 1 scallion (thinly sliced)
  • Crackers and pickles for serving

Steps:

  1. First, heat the cooking oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Then, sauté the minced garlic and shallots until fragrant. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Next, add the sliced meatballs or shrimp, if you're using them. Cook until they are cooked.
  4. Move the sautéed ingredients to the side of the wok. Crack the egg into the empty space and scramble it until it's cooked.
  5. After that, add the cooked rice to the wok. Mix all of the ingredients together thoroughly.
  6. Now, pour in the sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, ground pepper, and chicken powder (if using).
  7. Stir everything well until the rice changes color and the spices are evenly distributed.
  8. Remove the fried rice from the wok and sprinkle with thinly sliced scallions.
  9. Serve the fried rice with a fried egg, crackers, and pickles for a complete meal.
