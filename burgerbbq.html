How to Make BBQ Burger

Materials:

    Patty (Burger Meat)

  • 150 grams of ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ¼ teaspoon of black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • ½ tablespoon of butter (for cooking)

    • Toppings & Extras

  • 1 burger bun, split in half
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 2 fresh lettuce leaves
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 2 slices of onion (grilled briefly for sweetnes)
  • 2 tablespoons of BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • ½ tablespoon of ketchup

Steps:

  1. First, combine the ground beef with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and Worcestershire sauce (if using) in a bowl.
  2. Next, form the mixture into a patty (about 1.5 cm thick) to prevent it from drying out during cooking.
  3. Then, heat a skillet with a little butter. Carefully cook the patty for 3-4 minutes per side until it's fully cooked.
  4. After that, toast the burger bun briefly in the skillet until slightly crispy and not easily soggy.
  5. Now, spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of the bun
  6. Place the lettuce leaves on top, then add a slice of tomato.
  7. Put the cooked patty on top of the tomato, then immediately place the cheddar cheese slice so it melts.
  8. Add the grilled onions for a savory flavor.
  9. Spread BBQ sauce on top for a signature smoky BBQ taste.
  10. Finally, cover with the top half of the burger bun.
  11. Serve the BBQ burger with French fries or pickles as side dishes.
  12. Use ground beef with a little fat to keep the patty juicy.
  13. For a smokier flavor, grill the patty over charcoal or on a grill pan.
  14. Add crispy bacon for a richer taste.
